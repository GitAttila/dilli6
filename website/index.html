ABOUT US

From Dilli with love...

We believe there is no greater love then the love for food. Food is an eternal part of our lives and it dominates much of our daily conversations.

We at Dilli 6 Bistro and Bar are keen to provide true experience of Dilli 6, Our concept is inspired from the streets of Chandni chowk and Dilli 6 itself. It all started here, Famous Butter chicken, Chaat , street food culture , the famous Dal Makhani and you name it.

We are culinary Professionals, with over 8 years in the Industry and after exploring 3 continents and gathering great exposure in the field we are here to embark our journey on a new milestone.

Delicious Food , Signature Cocktails and a Unique Concept.

WHO WE ARE

Meet our team

Abhijeet

founder and chef

There is no greater love then the love of the food, and food dominates much of our daily conversations, for the years I have believed that the people who love food have been the best people to be with.

Travelled abroad, cooked almost in all kinds of kitchen, from domestic stove to state of the art. Those huge Indian gatherings, street food, fast food, mouth watering kebabs. Cooking for food lovers, cooking for food critics, it has been extraordinary. Been travelling, cooking and exploring for 8 years now, I feel great seeing people around me budding there love for food, and it has always been an immense pleasure cooking for them.
